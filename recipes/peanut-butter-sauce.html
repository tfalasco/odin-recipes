Peanut Butter Sauce

Photo by Kobby Mendez on Unsplash

Description

Who doesn't love peanut butter sauce on vanilla icecream? Add a drizzle of chocolate syrup and you have a Reese's-inspired icecream party!

This recipe is dead simple to make and can be whipped up in about a minute.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp corn syrup

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients. Combine the peanut butter and corn syrup in a small microwave-safe bowl or cup.
  2. Warm. Microwave for 20 seconds to warm up the mixture.
  3. Stir. Stir the mixture until it is smooth and creamy.
  4. Enjoy. Pour over icecream and indulge!
