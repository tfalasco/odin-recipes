Whipped Cream

Photo by Diliara Garifullina on Unsplash

Description

Whipped cream is the perfect accompaniment for many desserts, such as pies, ice cream, Belgian waffles, fruit, and frozen coffee drinks.

This recipe lets you tweak the flavor, sweetness, and consitency to your liking to get the exact creamy delight you are craving.

Ingredients

  • 1 c heavy whipping cream
  • 3 Tbsp powdered sugar, give-or-take to your liking
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional, can replace with other extracts)
  • A stand mixer or electric mixer with a whisk attachment.

Steps

  1. Pre-chill
    Place a large bowl and whisk in the freezer for 10 - 15 minutes to chill them. This will help the whipped cream come together much faster.
  2. Combine ingredients
    Combine the cream, sugar, and vanilla extract in the pre-chilled bowl.
  3. Whisk
    Rapidly whisk the cream on high for 3 - 5 minutes until soft peaks form or until you get your desired consistency.
  4. Enjoy
    Top your favorite dessert with this fresh whipped cream and indulge!
